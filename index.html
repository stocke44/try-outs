Good UX Design Saves Money

  • Good UX design reduces the cost of development cost down the line. When done well UX research will help identify the best ways to engage perspective customers .
  • A lack of research can lead to the development of featuresthat display content in a way that does not engage users and thus becoming a money pit.
  • The general rule of thumb is that for evry dollar invested in proper ui/ux design you save 10 to 100 dollars on your investment.

Understanding Demographics

  • In order to survive in this new era of instant content gratification . Companies must understand there target audience better than ever before. Part of this understanding is identifying how users view and absorb content.
  • Over 73% of consumers say content must be displayed well on the device they are using. (source: Adobe State of Content)
  • Over 66% of consumers value beautifully designed content over something that is simple and plain.
  • Customers Will stop engaging if the content they are trying to view does not display properly.
